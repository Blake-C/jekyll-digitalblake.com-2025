Be Something Amazing is a Drupal 7 career site aimed at helping visitors discover skilled-trade careers, and the team wanted both a visual refresh and a more engaging way to explore careers than a static page of links.

This was one of the rare projects where I owned both the design and the build, so I could make the visual direction and the interaction model reinforce each other. The anchor feature is the career finder: I used the Drupal API to pull a custom content type onto a single page and then wired MixItUp to filter and animate the career cards instantly, with no page reloads, so visitors can flick between categories and see options reshuffle in place. On the home page I worked with our video editor to weave a looping background video into the hero so the site feels alive the moment it loads, without pushing the introductory content below the fold.
